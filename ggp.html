Global Gathering Planner

Overview

Global Gathering is an annual retreat where the company takes all 30+ employees on a team building trip.

In response to Athlon Studio's remarkable growth over the past year, organizing the annual Global Gathering has become a complex coordination challenge. The HR team currently relies on manual spreadsheets to manage budgets, itineraries, travel documents, and more.

The core objective is to develop a cutting-edge tool that streamlines the Global Gathering planning process, alleviating the burden of manual tasks. This tool aims to enhance efficiency, empower the HR team, and optimize resource allocation for a seamless event experience.

Problem

1. Athlon Studio's annual Global Gathering demands heightened coordination due to the company's rapid growth in the past year.

2. The HR team currently relies on manual spreadsheets to manage diverse aspects of the event, including budgets, expenses, itineraries, traveller information, and visa requirements, leading to a heightened workload.

3. Explore the creation of an advanced tool designed to optimize the planning process, effectively tackling the challenges posed by manual management. This tool will empower the HR team to orchestrate the Global Gathering with enhanced efficiency and seamlessness.

Research

Competitor & Comparator Analysis

Through an extensive competitor & comparator analysis a diverse range of applications and websites such as Dropbox, Craft, Expedia, and others, we gained valuable insights into document gathering, event planning, and user preferences. By segmenting platforms based on their focus areas, we discerned commonalities in user flow and process. Notably, platforms like Planning Pod provided direct and clear steps, inspiring the aspiration for a comprehensive guideline within our design.

Our internal-use platform aims to consolidate various functionalities, addressing distinct needs and tasks not currently fulfilled by third-party platforms.

User Interviews

In the user interview analysis phase, we engaged two HR representatives and four employees to discern pain points and needs.

HR-focused inquiries unveiled the challenges they face in consistent communication and data collection. Our solution emerged as a platform allowing employees to build profiles and submit travel surveys, mitigating the need for constant individual outreach.

Meanwhile, employees expressed their desire for enhanced insight, transparent discussions, simplified communication, and easy access to itineraries.

This synthesis of perspectives has guided our design, ensuring our platform is tailored to address specific pain points and preferences for both HR representatives and employees.

User Personas & User Journeys

The creation of user personas and journeys breathed life into our research findings. As we visualized the challenges faced and the goals pursued by each persona. The importance of streamlined document gathering, simplified event planning, and traveler customization became clear. These research efforts helped us identify clear solutions required to create a well-rounded and useful Global Gathering Planner.

Solutions

1. Creating a platform that combines document gathering, consolidating scattered data and profile management into a single accessible hub.

2. Include features for sharing trips and inviting attendees, promoting collaboration and engagement, simplifying event planning, and fostering community among users.

3. Let travellers personalize their information and preferences, ensuring a user-centric experience, and minimizing the need for frequent interactions with the platform to just a few essential instances.

Ideation

Defining our initial concepts during the ideation phase was pivotal in shaping our design direction. Each concept targeted specific needs and audiences, providing clear pathways for development.

Concept 1: The Global Optimizer: Streamlining data tasks for admins, this concept focused on automation, quick data display, and centralized collection.

Concept 2: The Elite Planner: Aiming to expedite decisions, this concept integrated event creation, simplified location selection, and offered quick traveller profile views.

Concept 3: The Transparent Connector: Enhancing collaboration, this concept introduced a checklist for questions, shared plans among travellers, and provided up-to-date FAQs.

Concepts

Building upon our ideation, we refined concepts with user insights, resulting in subtle pivots. To offer a tangible glimpse into our platform, we crafted low-fidelity wireframes showcasing key screens and interactions. Visualizing the concepts allowed for client feedback on next steps and directions.

Concept 1

The Global Optimizer:

Addressing Athlon's intricate Gathering process, this concept introduces the Global Optimizer, a centralized repository for all event-related data. It tackles the challenge of data localization by providing easy access to files, user profiles, and form responses.

*Concept created by Camila

Concept 2

The Elite Planner:

Empowering HR with guided event planning, this concept centres on a dashboard designed for quick data display, filtering, and a streamlined procedure. Its user-friendly layout aims to enhance efficiency and clarity, guiding administrators through the planning process with ease.

*Concept created by Vina

Concept 3

MVP:

I crafted the third concept, focusing on user-centric efficiency enhancement. This concept optimizes the admin side of Global Gathering planning while ensuring a simplified experience for employees. It offers the advantage of a swift development timeline and a straightforward process, aligning closely with your vision.

Notably, it was selected to move forward by the client, demonstrating its alignment with their objectives.

Pros:

  • Short development timeline
  • Simplified process (admin & employees)
  • Reducing bloat software

Cons:

  • Small feature set
  • System limitations

MVP: Admin Profiles Page

This screen offers administrators a centralized hub to view completed traveler profiles. With filtering capabilities and the convenience of copying information for flight bookings and more. Additionally, they can upload essential documents like boarding passes and resort details, enabling travellers to access these crucial documents conveniently.

MVP: Traveller Profile Form

On the first screen when travellers entering the platform, this form simplifies the process for providing travel details and preferences to admins. The intuitive layout ensures easy data entry, empowering travellers to swiftly submit their information.

MVP: Traveller Itinerary

Following the creation of a traveller profile, this screen offers a countdown to the gathering that creates a sense of anticipation. The platform presents various tabs that cater to traveller engagement, including surveys related to activities at the event. When ready, the platform allows travellers to download their personalized itinerary, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable journey.

Variations

Following the root design elements of Concept 3, we crafted three different variations to cater to diverse user preferences and demographics. The first design focuses on a fun and playful color pallet and layout. The second variant took a minimalistic approach while maintaining Athlon Studio’s branding by incorporating colors and illustrations. The final variation focuses on the visual journey through past gatherings with pictures to generate anticipation and excitement among participants.

Variation 1

Playful Planner:

Global Gathering is an anticipated event for the Athlon Studio team. Like most team building virtual events, it represents a space for the team to spend quality time together outside the work space.

Playful Planner represents the feeling of participating in team bonding activities or seeing your teammates in person after a being remote for some time. With an emphasis on togetherness and play, this concept aims to be a digital expression of Athlon’s team building values.

*Concept created by Camila

Variation 2

Streamline Scheduler:

This concept focuses on presenting information as clearly as possible to travellers for making the planning process efficient.

The modern, clean interface shows relevant information displayed in a direct manner with minimal supporting graphics.

*Concept created by Vina

Variation 3

Picture-Perfect Planning:

Created by me, this variation has organized user profiles and makes information readily available, which minimizes employee dependency on admins, empowering travellers to efficiently manage their own preferences and travel details, contributing to a more streamlined and organized planning process.

Final Designs

In the final stages of the project, we merged the strengths of Concept 2 and Concept 3, informed by user feedback, to craft a cohesive and engaging user experience. Leveraging the minimalism and simplicity from Concept 2, we ensured a clean and intuitive interface. Additionally, we incorporated the delightful past gathering imagery from Concept 3, infusing an element of fun and nostalgia that resonated perfectly with our target audience, resulting in a comprehensive and compelling final design.
