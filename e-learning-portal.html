Athlon E-Learning Portal

Overview

As Athlon Studio undergoes rapid expansion, a crucial requirement emerged for a centralized learning hub to enhance onboarding and provide continuous learning resources. This rapid 2-week project aimed to create an accessible platform catering to employees' UX design education needs.Â 

This initiative addresses the company's growth demands, accentuating learning efficiency and exemplifying Athlon Studio's commitment to fostering a culture of ongoing personal and team development. The e-learning portal empowers employees to access UX/UI design resources at their convenience, contributing to a dynamic learning environment.

Problem

1. Athlon Studio's rapid growth makes it challenging to share knowledge and processes across the company, its employees and various communication channels.

2. New designers joining the team encounter challenges during onboarding as they are unfamiliar with Athlon Studio's design procedures.

3. There is a lack of centralized design standards or comprehensive documentation to set clear expectations for design work and skills at Athlon Studio.

Research

Internal Site Survey

One of the key findings from the internal survey was how employees learned about new tools within the organization. The results of this question shed light on a distinct gap between learning through formal onboarding and learning through discussions. While 50% of participants reported relying on company onboarding, a significant 95.5% emphasized the influential role of colleague-driven discussions in discovering new tools. This striking contrast highlights the need for enhanced onboarding efforts to bridge this gap.

Design Operations Card-Sorting

This initiative emerged as a pivotal research effort driven by one-on-one interviews with numerous employees. This exercise involved sorting lessons and identifying potential categories.

Some employee feedback received, including quotes such as "mini courses with different modules" and "...I need to know how to do this specific task," highlighted the demand for a centralized learning environment.

User Personas & User Journeys

The creation of user personas and journeys breathed life into our research findings. As we visualized the challenges faced and the goals pursued by each persona. The importance of streamlined document gathering, simplified event planning, and traveler customization became clear. These research efforts helped us identify clear solutions required to create a well-rounded and useful Global Gathering Planner.

Solutions

1. Creating a platform that combines document gathering, consolidating scattered data and profile management into a single accessible hub.

2. Include features for sharing trips and inviting attendees, promoting collaboration and engagement, simplifying event planning, and fostering community among users.

3. Let travellers personalize their information and preferences, ensuring a user-centric experience, and minimizing the need for frequent interactions with the platform to just a few essential instances.

Ideation

Defining our initial concepts during the ideation phase was pivotal in shaping our design direction. Each concept targeted specific needs and audiences, providing clear pathways for development.

Concept 1: The Global Optimizer: Streamlining data tasks for admins, this concept focused on automation, quick data display, and centralized collection.

Concept 2: The Elite Planner: Aiming to expedite decisions, this concept integrated event creation, simplified location selection, and offered quick traveller profile views.

Concept 3: The Transparent Connector: Enhancing collaboration, this concept introduced a checklist for questions, shared plans among travellers, and provided up-to-date FAQs.

Concepts

Building upon our ideation, we refined concepts with user insights, resulting in subtle pivots. To offer a tangible glimpse into our platform, we crafted low-fidelity wireframes showcasing key screens and interactions. Visualizing the concepts allowed for client feedback on next steps and directions.

Concept 1

The Global Optimizer:

Addressing Athlon's intricate Gathering process, this concept introduces the Global Optimizer, a centralized repository for all event-related data. It tackles the challenge of data localization by providing easy access to files, user profiles, and form responses.

*Concept created by Camila

Concept 2

The Elite Planner:

Empowering HR with guided event planning, this concept centres on a dashboard designed for quick data display, filtering, and a streamlined procedure. Its user-friendly layout aims to enhance efficiency and clarity, guiding administrators through the planning process with ease.

*Concept created by Vina

Concept 3

MVP:

I crafted the third concept, focusing on user-centric efficiency enhancement. This concept optimizes the admin side of Global Gathering planning while ensuring a simplified experience for employees. It offers the advantage of a swift development timeline and a straightforward process, aligning closely with your vision.

Notably, it was selected to move forward by the client, demonstrating its alignment with their objectives.

Pros:

  • Short development timeline
  • Simplified process (admin & employees)
  • Reducing bloat software

Cons:

  • Small feature set
  • System limitations

MVP: Admin Profiles Page

This screen offers administrators a centralized hub to view completed traveler profiles. With filtering capabilities and the convenience of copying information for flight bookings and more. Additionally, they can upload essential documents like boarding passes and resort details, enabling travellers to access these crucial documents conveniently.

MVP: Traveller Profile Form

The first screen travellers encounter upon entering the platform, this form simplifies the process of providing travel details and preferences. The intuitive layout ensures easy data entry, empowering travellers to swiftly submit their information.

MVP: Traveller Itinerary

Following the creation of a traveler profile, this screen offers a countdown to the gathering to create a sense of anticipation. The platform presents various tabs that cater to traveler engagement, including surveys related to activities at the event. When ready, the platform allows travellers to download their personalized itinerary, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable journey.
